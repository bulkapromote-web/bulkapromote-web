Case Study · oxanason.kz

English School
from Zero to Daily Leads

Oxana had been teaching English for years. Good reviews, word of mouth — that was it. Finding her school in Google was nearly impossible. A big city, plenty of competition, no website, invisible on maps, no ads.

We started from scratch.

Website. First thing — a real website: schedule, prices, online enrollment. Built on WordPress with fast load times and a clean mobile experience. No prospective student should leave because the page broke on their phone.

Maps and directories. We registered the school on Google Maps, Yandex Maps, and Google Business Profile. Photos, description, hours, website link — everything filled in. Now when someone types "English school Shymkent," Oxana shows up on the map.

SEO. We researched what locals actually search for, structured the pages, wrote content people actually read. Six months in — organic traffic up 40%.

Ads. We launched Google Ads and Yandex.Direct campaigns. Tested headlines, cut what wasn't working, kept what brought people in. Lead cost dropped 30% through optimization.

Result. Oxana gets inquiries straight to Telegram — with name, phone number, and question. Every month we send a report: where visitors came from, how many enrolled, what worked and what didn't.

FAQ

Digital Marketing,
SEO & Ads — FAQ

How much do your digital marketing services cost?

Pricing is individual — depends on the task, niche, and business scale. We study your request and propose the optimal solution for your budget. Contact us for a free consultation.

How long does it take to build a website?

Landing page: 7–14 days. Business website: 14–21 days. Online store: 21–30 days. Exact timeline agreed after requirements discussion.

Do you work with clients online worldwide?

Yes. Office in Shymkent, Kazakhstan, but we work remotely with clients worldwide — UAE, Saudi Arabia, Russia, and beyond. All communication and reports are online.

What does SEO optimization and promotion include?

Site audit, keyword research, technical optimization, content writing and publication, link building, and monthly reports on rankings and organic traffic.

How do I start working with you?

Contact us via Telegram, WhatsApp, or email. Free consultation, project analysis, work plan. Work starts within 48 hours of agreement.

Do you guarantee results from ads and SEO?

We work toward measurable KPIs: traffic, leads, cost-per-acquisition. Specific Google positions not guaranteed — those depend on the algorithm. All KPIs are defined before work begins.

Do you analyze competitors before starting?

Yes — it's the first step of every project. We research competitors' traffic, keywords, ad campaigns, and positioning. We find growth opportunities and untapped niches so you don't spend budget in the dark.

Do you create a marketing strategy / plan?

Always. Before launch we build a marketing strategy: goals, channels, budget, KPIs, timeline. We think first, then act — not the other way around.

Do you calculate ROI and unit economics?

Yes. We calculate cost per click, cost per lead, cost per customer, and projected revenue. You see the expected return before spending the first dollar. Numbers, not gut feelings.

Can I get a traffic and leads forecast before launch?

Yes. Based on niche data, we model expected traffic, lead volume, and cost per lead before the campaign starts. Transparent, with actual numbers — no "probably."

What reports do clients receive?

Monthly PDF report: traffic, sources, leads, cost per lead, ranking dynamics, budget spend, and next-month recommendations. New leads arrive in Telegram in real time — name, phone, and question.

Do you work with small businesses and startups?

Yes. Most of our clients are small and medium businesses. We match tools to actual budgets. If a project isn't economically viable — we'll say so honestly at the first consultation.
